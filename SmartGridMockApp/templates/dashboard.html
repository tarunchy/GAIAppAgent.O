How to Read the Data

The real-time grid data simulation shows...

  • Timestamp: The time at which the data was recorded.
  • Consumption (kWh): The amount of power consumed in kilowatt-hours.
  • Voltage (V): The voltage level of the grid.
  • Frequency (Hz): The frequency of the grid.
  • Power Factor: The efficiency of power usage.
  • Reactive Power (kVAR): The reactive power in kilovolt-amperes reactive.
  • Current (A): The current flowing through the grid in amperes.
